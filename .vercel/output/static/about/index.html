Whoami

I'm a UX Engineer Lead at a venture studio, where my sweet-spot is designing, coding and maintaining user interfaces amongst other responsibilities.

My tools of the trade are typically Typescript and React and plenty of CSS. From micro-interactions to strategically driven visual design, my goal is to create interfaces that not only look impressive, but feel effortless to use.

I'm an artist at heart and I approach my work with a unique perspective. I believe in breaking boundaries and creating the impossible.

Although I spend a large proportion of my time coding, what really gets me up in the morning is designing visually aesthetic and user-friendly interfaces. I find that my affinity for design combined with my understanding of the web enables me to create incredible websites that work as good as they look.

Experience and Education

I joined my present company in early 2022 where my responsibilities are designing and building the front-end of our various concepts. We build high potential Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) then spin them off into their own successful and independent ventures.

I earned my Bachelors of Science (B.S.) in Computer Science from the University of California, Riverside in 2022. During my time as a student at UCR, I devised and led the largest hackathon ever held in the California Inland Empire. Overseeing a team of ten, and under my leadership, this ambitious programming event succeeded in inspiring a host of up and coming engineers — something I'm still quite proud of.

After Hours

One of my favorite pastimes is photography — I love capturing the world around me the way I see it, whether I'm exploring new places or simply enjoying a regular walk through downtown.

I recently began playing the bass guitar and now enjoy playing and creating music in my spare time. There's something I find incredibly satisfying about the deep, rumble of a bass, and how it allows me to express myself in a completely different way.

Finally, I also enjoy taking scenic drives through canyons and other natural landscapes. It's peaceful being surrounded by nothing but silence and the open road.
